About Us

More Than Just A College Club. Learn More About Us!

MARS is a dynamic and vibrant club dedicated to fostering technical excellence, innovation, and personal growth among students. With a strong focus on technical events, national and international competitions, workshops, and self-development initiatives, we strive to create an environment that nurtures creativity, curiosity, and collaboration.

Membership at MARS is open to all students passionate about technology and innovation. Whether you're an aspiring engineer, a tech enthusiast, or a budding entrepreneur, MARS welcomes you to be a part of our thriving community. Be a part of something extraordinary and unleash your potential at MARS - where creativity, knowledge, and ambition converge to shape a better tomorrow.

Our Departments

CS-IT

Focuses on providing members with opportunities to develop and enhance their technical skills, engage in hands-on projects, and learn about current trends in technology. They organize events, workshops, and seminars.

Mechanical

Focuses on skill development, practical experience, and technical understanding in the field of mechanical engineering. They organize events like workshops, tutorials, competitions, and guest lectures to enhance students' understanding.

Electrical & ECE

Focuses on developing and enhancing technical skills, particularly in electrical engineering and related fields. They contribute by organizing and managing activities related to technical events, workshops, and student projects.

E&tc

Focuses on promoting technical knowledge and skills related to electronics and telecommunications. They aim to foster innovation, practical learning, and expose students to real-world problems through various activities.

Design and curation

Focuses on visual communication, aesthetics, and creative problem-solving. They handle tasks like creating logos, banners, flyers, and other promotional materials, as well as designing presentations and websites.

Finance and Legalities

Handles the financial and legal aspects of the club's operations. This includes managing the club's budget, ensuring financial compliance, handling legal documents, and advising on legal matters related to the club's activities.

Logistics and Event Management

Focuses on the practical, behind-the-scenes aspects of organizing and running events. They handle tasks like venue selection, budgeting, securing equipment, and coordinating staff and vendors to ensure events run smoothly.

Marketing and Public Relations

Focuses on building the club's image and outreach. They manage internal and external communication, promote the club's activities, and build relationships with the public and media.
